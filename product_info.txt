# Product Manual: Enterprise Smart Assistant v2.0

## Key Features
1. Automated customer query responses with multi-language support.
2. Generates business reports in PDF and Excel formats.
3. Semantic search for internal knowledge base.
4. Task reminders, calendar management, and meeting summarization.

## Use Cases
- Customer service teams: fast query resolution and FAQ automation.
- Sales teams: weekly reports, lead analysis, and pipeline tracking.
- HR teams: managing employee information, onboarding, and FAQs.

## System Requirements
- Operating System: Windows 10 / macOS 12 / Linux
- RAM: 8GB minimum
- Network: Stable internet connection for cloud-based AI queries.

## Release Notes
- 2025-Q1: Added multi-language support for English, Spanish, and Mandarin.
- 2025-Q2: Enhanced knowledge base search accuracy and response times.
- 2025-Q3: Integrated calendar and task management module.
