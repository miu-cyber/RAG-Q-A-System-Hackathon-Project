Q1: What is the onboarding process?
A: New hires complete online registration, submit personal documents, attend orientation sessions, and sign employment and confidentiality agreements.

Q2: What are the salary and benefits?
A: The company provides a base salary, performance bonus, health insurance, retirement plans, and holiday benefits. Employees are also eligible for annual health checkups and professional development allowances.

Q3: How does training and promotion work?
A: Employees are required to complete at least one training session per quarter. High performers may apply for promotions, which involve assessments, interviews, and manager recommendations.

Q4: What is the leave policy?
A: Employees are entitled to paid annual leave, sick leave, personal leave, and family leave. Specific leave policies are outlined in the employee handbook.

Q5: How can I access HR support?
A: HR support can be accessed through the internal portal, email, or by booking one-on-one meetings with HR representatives.
